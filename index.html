Our Products

We specialize in the sourcing, processing, and export of premium quality seeds and spices. Each product undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets international standards.

Fennel Seeds

Premium quality fennel seeds (saunf) known for their sweet, aromatic flavor. Ideal for culinary, medicinal, and industrial applications. Available in bulk quantities with consistent quality.

Carom Seeds

High-grade carom seeds (ajwain) with a distinctive, pungent aroma. Sourced from trusted farms and processed under strict quality control. Perfect for food processing and pharmaceutical industries.

Coriander Seeds

Carefully selected coriander seeds with a rich, citrusy flavor profile. Our coriander seeds meet international quality standards and are suitable for spice blends, oil extraction, and food manufacturing.

Cumin Seeds

Premium cumin seeds with an earthy, warm flavor. Essential for spice blends and culinary preparations worldwide. Available in various grades to meet diverse client requirements.

Turmeric Powder

Pure, vibrant turmeric powder with high curcumin content. Rich golden color and authentic aroma, perfect for culinary use, food coloring, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical applications.

Red Chillies

Bold, fiery dried red chillies sourced from the finest regions. Available whole or ground, offering consistent heat levels (mild to extra hot) and deep red color for spices, sauces, and food processing.

Black Pepper

Premium bold black peppercorns with intense aroma and sharp pungency. Carefully processed to retain essential oils. Widely used as the "King of Spices" in global cuisines and seasoning blends.
